Brutus's Movie List

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Fifth Element

The direction and editing make this film a masterpiece. I love how beautifully Luc Besson weaves the dialogue from one shot to interact with the next one. It's loads of fun and endlessly quotable.

Lord of the Rings

Yes, I know it's three movies and I do mean the extended edition. It's a brilliant adaptation of three equally brilliant novels. It has some flaws, but I adore it.

Dead Poet's Society

This movie touched me deeply. It is a wonderful ode to life itself and celebrates how amazing, complex, thrilling, difficult and ephemeral it is.
